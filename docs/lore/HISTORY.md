# 📜 The History of ChadVis

## Phase 1: The Dream (Jan 2026)
It started with a simple idea: projectM v4 is amazing, but the existing GUIs were... lacking. They weren't "Chad" enough. They didn't have the C++20 power.

## Phase 2: The Unholy Trinity
We integrated Suno AI and FFmpeg. Suddenly, you could generate music, visualize it, and record it all in one app. The "Arch BTW" community noticed.

## Phase 3: The Modern Era (Today)
With Sidebar navigation, hardware acceleration, and now, modular documentation, ChadVis has reached its final form. Or has it?
